Functionality

In this project, I tackled the Digits dataset by testing four different models - Logistic Regression (multiclass classifier), SVM, KNN, and Linear Regression. I meticulously split the data into training, validation, and test sets, with 60% of the data dedicated to training, 20% to validation, and 20% to testing. I also played around with hyperparameters for all the models except the linear model. Finally, I compared the accuracy of each model to determine which one was the most effective.
