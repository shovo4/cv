Functionality

This application is designed to serve five different user types, each with unique roles and permissions: Admin, Content Creator, Teacher, Student, and Parent.

The Content Creator is responsible for uploading content that will be used in the app. Teachers can assign lessons to students, including the option to set repetitions to ensure mastery. Students then practice the assigned lessons by reading the text and listening to the audio, and they can mark the lesson as complete once they have mastered it.

After the student completes a lesson, the teacher reviews their work and confirms the completion or requests that the student redo the lesson with additional repetitions. Additionally, parents can track the progress of their child (the student) and monitor their performance within the app.

By catering to the needs of these different user types, this application offers a comprehensive solution for improving the learning experience of students while providing teachers and parents with the tools they need to ensure success.
