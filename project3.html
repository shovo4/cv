Functionality

NoGame: Introducing NoGame, a dynamic and engaging game that puts you in control. With customizable board sizes and the ability to display the board at any time, NoGame offers endless possibilities for players of all levels. Whether you prefer black or white stones, NoGame has something for everyone. This game features legal move options for each player, allowing for a fully interactive and immersive experience. And with the ability to autoplay and resign when needed, NoGame is both fun and practical. Overall, NoGame is a must-play for anyone looking to enjoy a thrilling and engaging game experience. So why wait? Dive in today and discover all the excitement NoGame has to offer.

GoGame: Introducing Go game, the exciting Go game that offers two powerful selection methods: RR and UCB. With the RR selection method, you can explore nodes that have high win rates and low visit counts, giving you the advantage you need to win. And with the UCB selection method, you can balance exploration and exploitation to select nodes with the highest potential for reward. But that's not all. Go game also offers policy random and policy pattern options, allowing you to choose between random legal moves with probabilities or possible legal moves with compared probabilities. So what are you waiting for? Experience the thrill of Go game and take your gaming skills to the next level today!
