Functionality

StudyUp is a Student Activity Tracker application that lets the users keep track of their progress, grades, deadlines, and discussion posts in any particular course that they may be enrolled in. This application does not let participants enrol or drop courses, that process takes place on a different platform, this application will act as a supplement to that platform.
