Functionality

QR Hunter is an exciting mobile app that allows users to sign up, explore maps, and scan QR codes to earn points. With the option to scan codes both virtually and face to face, players can engage in an immersive experience with a range of fun challenges. The app also features a leaderboard, enabling users to compare their scores with others and compete for the top spot. Additionally, users can save and share images, generate QR tokens of their profiles, and log out of their accounts for added security. With its user-friendly interface and engaging features, QR Hunter is an ideal choice for anyone looking for a thrilling gaming experience.
