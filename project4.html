Functionality

Introducing the Keeper app, that takes task management to the next level! With this app, you can add and remove tasks instantly, allowing you to stay organized and focused on what matters most. And the best part? There's no need for a backend, which means you can start using the app right away without worrying about setting up a server or database. However, it's important to note that tasks will not be saved if you refresh the page, so be sure to finish your tasks before closing the app. Whether you're a busy professional or a student with a lot on your plate, the Keeper app is the perfect tool for keeping track of your tasks and staying productive. So why wait? Try the Keeper app today and take control of your to-do list like never before!
